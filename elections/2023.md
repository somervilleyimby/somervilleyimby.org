A note about rent stabilization: members of Somerville YIMBY have a wide range of opinions about the usefulness of rent stabilization measures. Economic research and history show us that price controls can impede badly needed new construction. For example, Saint Paul, MN enacted a 3% maximum rent increase regulation and saw construction plummet, exacerbating their housing shortage.
